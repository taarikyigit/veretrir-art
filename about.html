How to deploy

1. Upload your images
Use FTP, Cpanel File Manager, or your hosting's drag-and-drop uploader.
Put artwork images in images/ and 3D models in images/3d/.

2. Edit your content
In the panels on the left, add/edit your artworks, poems, and articles. Use the exact same image path you uploaded (e.g. images/work1.jpg).

3. Export and upload data.js
Click Export data.js in the top bar. Download the file, then upload it to your site root — replacing the old data.js.

4. Upload site files
On first setup, upload ALL these files to your site root:
index.html   artworks.html   writings.html
about.html   contact.html   admin.html
style.css   common.js   data.js

5. That's it.
After that, you only need to re-upload data.js + your image files whenever you add new work.
