How to use

  1. Choose a type: Artwork, Poem, Article, or Book
  2. Fill in the form and click Generate
  3. Click Copy and paste into the matching list in content.js
  4. New entries go at the top of the list, right after the opening [
  5. Save content.js and reload — done.
